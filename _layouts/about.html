About Jackie

Over 10 Years in Government Contracting

I have over 10 years of Federal Contracting experience with the Department of Defense (DoD), NASA and the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA). I have also worked in private industry and therefore understand the difficulties your company may face when looking for work and responding to solicitations.

I am considered a subject matter expert among my peers and senior leadership in the field of federal acquisition, specifically in service, cost reimbursement and firm fixed price type contracts. I have also developed procurement policy for overseas contingency contracts in Iraq and Afghanistan.

Throughout my successful career with the Federal Government, I negotiated and provided oversight of various multi-million dollar contracts for mission-critical needs which directly support the Army, NASA and the National Security Agency (NSA). I counseled key Government decision makers regarding complex contractual arrangements to support their ongoing mission while saving taxpayer money.

I earned my MBA in 2003 from Loyola. I am Level III DAWIA certified in Government Contracting and was recently awarded the 2011 Secretary of the Army Award for Excellence in Contracting in the Category of Outstanding Procurement Analyst.
